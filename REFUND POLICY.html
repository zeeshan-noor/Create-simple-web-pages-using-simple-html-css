REFUNDS

After receiving your return/exchange request, we will process your return or exchange. We do our best to cater to any refund/return requests timely. Refunds may take 1-2 billing cycles to appear on your credit card statement, depending on your credit card company. We will notify you by email when your return has been processed. The refund conditions are as follow;

If the tutor canceled a lesson:

All the program values will be returned to the student wallet. The student will be charged for the lessons at reduced rates by Face2Face if they have selected a program.

If the student cancels a lesson/program:

If the program is canceled 24 hours before the commencing of the lesson/program, all the value will be returned to the student wallet. If a program is canceled within 24 hours to 6 hours before commencing, Face2Face holds the right to charge a percentage of the total program cost as a cancellation fee. In case of cancellation after the commencing of a lesson/program, the student will be charged for each lesson cost individually.

EXCEPTIONS

Face2Face holds the right to accept/deny your refund request at any point in time with no prior notification.
